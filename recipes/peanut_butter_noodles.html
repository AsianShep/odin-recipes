Peanut Butter Noodles

Ingredients

  • Favorite noodles
  • Scallions
  • Soy sauce
  • Peanut butter
  • Sesame oil

Steps

  1. Put noodles in pot and boil until soft
  2. While noodles are boiling combine soysauce, peanut butter, and sesame oil in a bowl
  3. Combines noodles with sauce and top with scallions
